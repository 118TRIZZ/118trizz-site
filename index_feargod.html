About 118trizz

At 118trizz, we create soaps that reflect comfort, creativity, and care. Our small-batch bars are handcrafted to nourish your skin and elevate your day. Every scent is inspired by something real and meaningful.

Our History

118trizz is more than a brand — it's a story. “118” comes from my mom’s house in Syracuse, NY. That home inspired the love and warmth we try to bring into every product. “Trizz” is my own personal touch — a tag that’s been with me through the journey.
