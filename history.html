The Story Behind 118trizz

Our name, 118trizz, carries deep personal roots. It began with a house — 118 — a warm, welcoming home in Syracuse, NY where my mom lived and raised our family. That address became more than just a number; it represented love, comfort, and fresh beginnings — the same values we pour into every bar of soap we make.

And Trizz? That’s my personal tag. A nickname, a vibe, a reminder to stay true to myself and my creative spark. Together, 118trizz isn’t just a brand. It’s a story — of where we come from and where we’re going. One bar of soap at a time.
