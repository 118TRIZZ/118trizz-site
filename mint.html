Minty Fresh

Minty Fresh is a cool, refreshing soap bar infused with real peppermint oil. Its cooling sensation awakens your skin and refreshes your senses, making it ideal for a morning boost. Formulated with olive oil and coconut oil to deeply cleanse without drying. Naturally antibacterial and perfect for hot summer days or post-workout showers. Its minty aroma lingers lightly on the skin, leaving you feeling revitalized. Crafted in small batches with eco-conscious ingredients and zero synthetic colors. Lathers richly and rinses clean, leaving no residue. Vegan and palm-oil free. Recommended for normal to oily skin. Store dry to extend life. Net weight ~4.5oz.
